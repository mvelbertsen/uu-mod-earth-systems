solver.physics.StokesContinuitySolver¶

Stokes and Continuity equations solver.

Uses scipy’s sparse arrays and sparse direct matrix solver to solve the Stokes and Continuity equations in 2D.

Functions

S_to_grid_Stokes(S, xres, yres, Pscale, BC)

Translates the solution of the linear system back to spatial grids for each of vx, vy ,P.

StokesConstructMatrix(xnum, ynum, xstp, ...)

Constructs the matrix and RHS vector system for solving the Stoke's + continuity eqns.

StokesContinuitySolver(eta_s, eta_n, xnum, ...)

This function formulates and solves Stokes and Continuity equations defined on 2D staggered irregularly spaced grid with specified resolution (xnum, ynum) and grid lines positions (gridx, gridy) given distribution of right parts for all equations (RX,RY,RC) on the grid and given variable shear (etas) and normal (etan) viscosity distributions pressure is normalized relative to given value (prnorm) in the first cell

calculateResiduals(xnum, ynum, xstp, xstpc, ...)

Calculates the residuals of the Stoke's solver.

constructStokesRHS(grid, grid0, params, ...)

Computes the RHS (spatial domain) vectors for the vx, vy and continuity eqns.
